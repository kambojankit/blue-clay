SERVICES

ONLINE


WEB DESIGN
We create hand-crafted websites, incorporating our clients' brands into our artwork. Our extensive experience helps us design for any project, regardless of size or industry.
DIGITAL STRATEGY
We deliver elegant and powerful solutions to help our clients thrive on the web. We create and execute marketing strategies for long term growth, whether you're a startup or a Fortune 500 company.
ECOMMERCE
We help clients sell their products online through beautiful and effective online shopping solutions. Let us get your product or service online and selling!
MOBILE PLATFORMS
We design and develop platforms for all things mobile. From iPhone applications to Facebook Widgets, we do it all. We can help bring your existing brand into the world of mobile media, or help launch your new concept from scratch.
SOCIAL MEDIA
We help our clients extend their presence to Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Linkedin and more. Reach your demographic and increase your marketing efforts through the power of social media!

OFFLINE


BRAND IDENTITY
We establish your brand by learning all about your product, demographic and vision. We then create that identity by unifying specific colors, graphics, and typefaces to increase your brand recognition.
LOGO DESIGN
The single most important part of a brand identity. Cleverbird has successfully created many unique and effective logos for our clients, and look forward to creating the right logo to represent your brand.
PRINT DESIGN
There’s something about a tangible piece of design that’s so special. We would love to portray your brand through print; from business cards to brochures, we can help!
PRESENTATION
Gone are the days of boring powerpoints! Creating professional presentation materials is a specialty of ours. Let us breathe life into your next sales presentation, tradeshow booth, or infographic.
